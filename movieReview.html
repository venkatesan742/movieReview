STORY

Rocketry(The Nambi Effect) is based on the life of Nambi Narayanan. Nambi Narayanan's character is played by Madhavan. The Story tells how is Nambi Narayan, and what he did for our nation. He is Rocket Scientist in Indian Space Research Organisation. Manufacturing of Liquid Engine is started in France to lunch various rockets in earth's orbit. So, there required scientists for the proper design of an engine. Nambi Narayanan and his team went to France and learned a technique that how to design a liquid engine. This helps very much while designing Vikas engine.

RELEASE

Madhavan initially announced that the film would be theatrically released on 1 April 2022 in six languages(Hindi, Tamil, English, Malayalam, Telugu, and Kannada). However, the release date was postponed. Finally, the film was released theatrically worldwide on 1 July 2022.

DISTRIBUTION

The Film was distributed worldwide by Yash Raj Films and Phars Film Co, with UFO Moviez additionally acquiring the film's distribution rights in North India. The Film"s Tamil Version was distributed by Udhayanidhi Stalin's Red Gaint Movies in Tamilnadu, and AGS Cinemas joined as the official multiplex partner.
